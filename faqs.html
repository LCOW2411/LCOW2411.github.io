Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What makes FineCraftSlice cutting boards unique?

Our cutting boards are handcrafted from the finest materials, designed to last a lifetime. Each board is uniquely crafted to ensure both durability and beauty, making every piece a functional work of art.

How do I care for my FineCraftSlice cutting board?

To ensure the longevity of your board, we recommend hand washing with mild soap and water and drying immediately. Periodically oiling your board with food-grade mineral oil will also help maintain its natural luster.

Can I customize my cutting board?

Yes, we offer customization options for our cutting boards. You can choose the size, wood type, and even add a personal engraving to make your board truly one-of-a-kind.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally. Shipping costs vary based on destination and order size. Please contact us for more information regarding international orders.

What is your return policy?

We stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a replacement or full refund, excluding custom orders.
